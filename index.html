Nudge — Privacy Policy

Simple. Local-first. No tracking.

Last updated: [insert date]

1. Information We Collect

Nudge does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. All app activity (timers, settings, intervals) stays entirely on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

If Nudge saves preferences (such as timer intervals or theme), these are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

3. Internet and Third‑Party Services

Nudge operates fully offline and does not connect to the internet or use third‑party analytics, cloud storage, or tracking services.

4. Permissions

Nudge may request permission to:

  • Show fullscreen overlays or notifications (so it can remind you to take breaks)
  • Run in the background (so reminders trigger even when the app is minimized)

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and never for data collection.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

Nudge is suitable for all ages and does not collect or process any data from users, including children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be published here with a new "Last updated" date. Any future changes will prioritize user privacy and data safety.
